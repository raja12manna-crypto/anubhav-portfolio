Who I am

Designer who asks why before how.

I'm Anubhav Manna — a Product Designer and UX Researcher based in Bengaluru with a background in Visual Communication and a Master's in Experience Design from NIFT Bengaluru.

My work lives at the intersection of human-centred design, interaction design, and systems thinking. I've led end-to-end design across B2B SaaS dashboards, drone mission control interfaces, brand identities, and motion design — always with research at the core.

Outside of screens: I play guitar, swim, sketch while travelling, and occasionally argue that serif fonts deserve more respect in product design.
